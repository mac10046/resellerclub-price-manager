Why invest in a Wealthbasket?

A wealthbasket is a basket of stocks which adheres to an underlying theme. Currently, the Indian market is flooded with products that are largely similar in nature and thus, are limited in their ability to provide true portfolio diversification. Additionally, due to the current structure of the markets, the availability of innovative products is restricted to institutional/UHNI investors, leaving retail investors with limited options for portfolio diversification. Wealthbasket give all investors, irrespective of size or investment styles, an opportunity to invest in intelligent investment ideas in a low cost and transparent manner.
